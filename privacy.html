Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Discussed respects your privacy and wants you to understand how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Service. This Privacy Policy explains our practices. By using Discussed, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, age, gender, location preferences, topics of interest, and any other profile information you choose to provide.

1.2 Authentication Data: We receive identifiers and tokens from third-party providers such as Google or Facebook if you use them to log in.

1.3 Usage Data: We collect information about your activity on the Service, such as conversations you start, search preferences, device type, operating system, and app version.

1.4 Location Data: With your permission, we collect approximate or precise location data to power search and discovery features. You can revoke location permissions at any time via your device settings, but some features may not function.

1.5 Content: We store messages, photos, and other content you share to operate the Service. Encrypted backups may be stored in Firebase services.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 To operate, maintain, and improve the Service, including matching users and recommending conversations.

2.2 To secure the Service, detect fraud, enforce policies, and prevent harmful behavior.

2.3 To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, or customer support requests.

2.4 To personalize your experience, including showing relevant topics, suggestions, or promotions.

2.5 To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 With other users when you choose to share profile details, messages, photos, or participate in group chats.

3.2 With service providers that perform functions on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment processing. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

3.3 With law enforcement or regulators when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Discussed, our users, or the public.

3.4 In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality safeguards.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law or to enforce our rights.

4.2 We may retain aggregated, non-identifiable information after account deletion for analytics and reporting.

5. Your Choices

5.1 You can update your profile information and preferences within the app. Some updates may be subject to cooldown periods to protect community integrity.

5.2 You can manage notifications and permissions through your device settings.

5.3 You can request deletion of your data by deleting your account or contacting us at privacy@discussed.app.

6. Children

Discussed is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us to remove it.

7. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of information to locations where Discussed or its service providers operate.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@discussed.app.
