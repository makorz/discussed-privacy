Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in to Discussed with Facebook (or any other provider) and want to delete your data or account, follow the steps below.

Delete your Discussed account (recommended)

Deleting your account removes your profile, messages, and other data from Discussed.

  1. Open the Discussed app.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon or menu).
  3. Find and tap Delete account (or similar).
  4. Confirm deletion. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

After you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must keep it by law.

Request data deletion by email

If you cannot access the app or want to request deletion another way:

  • Email us at privacy@discussed.app with the subject line “Data deletion request”.
  • Include the email address or Facebook account you used to sign up (if you remember it).
  • We will process your request and delete your data as described in our Privacy Policy.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we remove or anonymize:

  • Your profile (name, photo, description, preferences)
  • Your messages and chat history
  • Other data we hold that identifies you

We may keep aggregated, non-identifiable information for analytics as allowed by law.

Contact

For privacy or data requests: privacy@discussed.app
